+ Actors 

Student / Learner

Enrolls in courses

Accesses lessons, quizzes, and assignments

Tracks progress and grades

Interacts in forums or discussions

Instructor / Teacher

Creates and manages courses

Uploads content (videos, PDFs, exercises)

Creates quizzes, assignments, and exams

Reviews student submissions and grades

Admin / System Administrator

Manages user accounts (students, instructors)

Manages platform content

Handles reporting and analytics

Manages payments, subscriptions, and permissions
Payment Gateway

Handles course payments and subscriptions

Confirms transaction success/failure

Email / Notification Service

Sends notifications to students and instructors

Sends reminders for assignments, exams, or events

External Content Provider (Optional)

Supplies additional learning resources (e.g., e-books, videos)

Learning Management System (LMS) Integration (Optional)

Allows importing/exporting courses or grades between systems
